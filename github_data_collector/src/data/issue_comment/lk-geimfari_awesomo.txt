
Moonscript is a pretty cool language written in itself and Lua designed to compile to Lua. Surprised this wasn't on the list before since we have leafo's Lapis, which is written partly in MoonScript.

I think this would be a great addition because it allows you to add maps to your javascript/Node app without having to deal with all the pain and agony of a full mapping framework. You can simply define your map style in json, pass it to the api, and you get a png you can embed. Pretty awesome!
