### What is Rawsec's CyberSecurity Inventory?

An inventory of tools and resources about CyberSecurity. This inventory aims to help people to find everything related to CyberSecurity.

![](https://i.imgur.com/iLNgnlz.png)

More details about features [here](https://inventory.rawsec.ml/features.html).

Note: the inventory is a FLOSS (Free, Libre and Open-Source Software) project.

### Why should you care about being inventoried?

Mainly because this is giving visibility to your tool and improve its referencing.

### Badges

The badge shows to your community that your are inventoried. It looks good but also shows you care about your project, that your tool is referenced.

Feel free to claim your badge here: http://inventory.rawsec.ml/features.html#badges, it looks like that [![Rawsec's CyberSecurity Inventory](https://inventory.rawsec.ml/img/badges/Rawsec-inventoried-FF5050_flat.svg)](https://inventory.rawsec.ml/), but there are several styles available.
